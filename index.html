About

As an End-of-Life Doula, I play a vital role in providing comfort, guidance, and companionship to individuals and families who are facing the end of life. I work closely with families to understand their needs, values, and beliefs, providing holistic support that encompasses emotional, spiritual, and practical guidance.

My goal is to ensure that the dying person and their loved ones feel supported, heard, and empowered during this challenging time. I provide education and resources to help families navigate the dying process, make informed decisions about end-of-life care, and offer support and guidance during the bereavement process. My extensive training, along with my full-time presence and companionship during vigil and at death, my advocacy, and support for physical, practical, and life review needs, represent a service not widely offered elsewhere.
